Ammorra. Reproducing Reproduction.

Pregnancy? Unhealthy women? Unhealthy babies? Infertility? Not with Ammorra.

Blogs

Our goal is to make menstruation and pregnancy a much more healthy, safe and inclusive process.

There are so many curses that come with the blessing to reproduce. The current proccess is unnecessarily inefficient. From not knowing about what's going on in your body, to the gess and check proccess of getting pregnant and the many complications and diseases that come with it. We want to 1. provide females with new options for monitoring pre-partum, during pregnancy, and post-partum, 2. provide artificial wombs, artificial sperms and monitoring for fertility.

Monitoring

Using nanosensors to test for vital diseases that a mother may be susceptible to during pregnancy. Nanoswimmers would be used as molecular delivery vehicles to work as treatment methods for any reproductive diseases/illnesses detected by nanosensors.

Fertility

Allowing for the option of creating lab grown artificial sperms and eggs to create an embryo using stem cells. The embryo would be sustained in an artificial womb. This process would produce a healthy baby and help infertile, or working moms.

More effecient pregnancy

This will give women more options for fertility and allow them to become more aware of what's going on in their bodies so that they can treat complications quicker. This will ensure a healthy pre-partum, pregnancy and post-partum.

Developement of nanosensors used to test for vital diseases and complications during pregnancy. A system to attach Nanoswimmers on egg to guide a healthy sperm to egg.

Nanosensors and Nanoswimmers

Nanoswimmers would be used in the blood stream as the treatment methods. An IoT system developed to be able to monitor hormonal levels, vitimans etc. in your body.

Nanoswimmers and Monitoring

Artificial sperms and eggs developed using stem cells to form an embryo in lab. After 3-4 weeks, the fetus would enter and be sustained in an artificial womb.
